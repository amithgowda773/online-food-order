Our Story

A family legacy of flavour, spice, and hospitality since 2010.

14+ Years of Excellence

From a Family Kitchen to Bangalore's Favourite

Zafrani began in 2010 as a humble dhaba run by the Sharma family in Yelahanka, Bangalore. Driven by a passion for authentic recipes passed down through generations, we turned a modest dream into one of the city's most loved culinary destinations.

The name Zafrani — derived from the Urdu word for saffron — represents the golden spice that defines our cooking philosophy: precious, aromatic, and transformative. Every dish we serve carries the warmth of home and the richness of India's diverse culinary heritage.

Today, we serve over 500 happy customers daily, operating both our dine-in restaurant and online delivery platform — bringing the same warmth and quality to your doorstep.
